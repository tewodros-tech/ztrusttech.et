Who We Are

Built Through Experience & Execution

Over the years, ZTrust Tech has grown from technical service delivery into a full-spectrum IT solutions partner — trusted by enterprises across Ethiopia and beyond.

From Vision to Enterprise Reality

Founded in 2016, ZTrust Tech began with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between complex enterprise IT needs and reliable, world-class technical delivery. What started as a focused team of certified engineers has evolved into Ethiopia's most trusted full-spectrum IT partner.

Today, we design, deploy, and maintain the digital infrastructure that powers banks, telcos, government institutions, and enterprises — handling everything from hyperscale data centers to mission-critical application testing.

Our growth has been driven by one principle: deliver uncompromising quality, every time. This has earned us the trust of 120+ enterprise clients and positioned us as a leader in East Africa's IT services landscape.

With a team of 50+ certified professionals and a 24/7 NOC, we don't just deliver projects — we engineer long-term technology partnerships that fuel our clients' growth.
