Our History

From a small gathering to a global movement — God's faithfulness through the years

1988

The Humble Beginning

A small group of believers gathered under a tree in Yerer, Ethiopia, hungry for the Word and the presence of the Holy Spirit. Led by a visionary pastor, they prayed for revival.

1995

First Church Building

With sacrificial giving and community support, the first church building was constructed. The congregation grew to 200 members, becoming a beacon of hope in the region.

2005

Expansion & Global Vision

The church launched its first mission outreach, sending teams to neighboring regions. The vision expanded beyond Yerer, embracing a heart for the nations.

2015

Digital Ministry Begins

Embracing technology, Yerer Full Gospel began live-streaming services, reaching Ethiopians in the diaspora and beyond. The message of salvation went global.

2024

A Multi-National Family

Today, we are a thriving church of over 1,200 members from 38 nations. Our campus continues to expand, and our heart remains: worship in Spirit and truth.

Our Mission

To make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey all that Christ commanded. We are committed to worship in Spirit and truth, empowered by the Holy Spirit, and driven by love for God and people.

Evangelism Discipleship Community

Our Vision

To see a generation of Spirit-filled believers who impact their communities, transform nations, and glorify God through worship, prayer, and the proclamation of the Full Gospel. We envision Yerer Full Gospel as a lighthouse of hope, raising leaders and planting churches across Ethiopia and the world.
