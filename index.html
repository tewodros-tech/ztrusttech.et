About Cambridge Academy Ethiopia

Excellence in education, rooted in Ethiopian values with a global perspective

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Cambridge Academy Ethiopia has grown from a small primary school to one of Addis Ababa's premier international schools. We serve over 1,000 students from nursery through A-Levels, representing more than 20 nationalities.

Our mission is to provide a world-class British education that prepares students for success in a rapidly changing global society while fostering a deep appreciation for Ethiopian culture and heritage.

Our Vision

To be the leading British curriculum school in East Africa, producing compassionate, innovative, and resilient global citizens who make positive contributions to their communities and the world.

Our Values

  • Academic Excellence
  • Integrity & Respect
  • Innovation & Creativity
  • Community Engagement
  • Global Citizenship
